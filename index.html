January 28th, A Night For Us

Rachel and Natalie invite you to join in the celebration as they exchange vows and party the night away.

Where

Sole Repair Shop
1452 10th Ave
Seattle, WA 98122

As scandalous and elusive as it sounds, you need to look for the white door. There are no signs or an address out front, but there is a door man.

Please plan to arrive at 6pm sharp. Ceremony will begin shortly after. Reception to follow.

What to Expect

The ceremony will be held downstairs. There will be some reserved seating for family up front, the rest will be open. We also encourage those who don't mind standing to head upstairs to the loft for a better view of the ceremony. We will love you forever if you take a lot of photos/video.

Following the ceremony, downstairs will be transformed into dancing and karaoke. We encourage you to sing as many karaoke songs as possible, bonus points for upbeat and danceable, or super cheesy and romantic.

If karaoke isn't your thing upstairs will be the place for you. More chill and lounge feeling, with an Xbox/Kinect playing Dance Central and Michael Jackson Experience.

Parking

Parking is immediately to the south of the venue. It is a pay lot that accepts credit/debit cards. You may get lucky and find street parking, but I wouldn't count on it. If you live close, a bus or cab may be your best bet. If you're feeling fancy, there is always Uber Car or Crown Black Car Service.

Food + Drink

There will be tasty hors d'oeuvres, and partially hosted beer + wine. Should you have a taste for mixed drinks there will be a cash bar. We encourage you to eat dinner prior to arriving. Also, there is a rumor there will be a midnight run to Dick's Burgers.

Kids

Kids are absolutely welcome and encouraged to attend, just keep in mind that anyone under 21 needs to leave by 10pm. So if you want to party the night away, hiring a sitter may be a good idea.

Registry

In an attempt to move away from the norm, family and friends are encouraged to donate to the Honeymoon Fund. The plan is to spend our time relaxing in Hawaii (we've never been!) On the to-do list: hiking, volcano tours, surfing, etc. Also taking suggestions!

PayPal is the easiest, most familiar way to send money online. We encourage you to use the "other" option under personal payments to avoid charges. Email address is natalie@otakuchick.com.

If you would rather send a more traditional gift, we are registered at Crate and Barrel.

Hotels
